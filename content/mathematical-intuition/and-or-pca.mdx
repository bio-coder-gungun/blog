We all use 'and' and 'or' fairly frequently in our speech and they don't seem to imply any specific order. 

For example, if I say stretch and rotate it doesn't seem like I am saying you have to stretch first and then rotate or the inverse. 

But in specific context it would matter because the order of the events changes things completely.

The source of this confusion started when I started revising linear algebra with the wonderful videos from 3Blue1Brown.
